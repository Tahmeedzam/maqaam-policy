# Privacy Policy

**Maqaam: Companion for Believers**

Last updated: 19-06-2026

## Introduction

Maqaam ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Maqaam is a personal prayer-journey companion. There are no leaderboards, no public profiles, and no comparison features — your data belongs to you.

## Information We Collect

**Account Information**
When you sign up, we collect your email address and, if you choose to sign in with Google, your name and profile picture as provided by Google.

**Location Data**
We use your device's location to:
- Show nearby mosques on the map
- Detect when you are near a mosque you haven't visited, so we can prompt you to confirm your visit
- Calculate the distance between you and nearby mosques

Location data is used only while the app is active or briefly in the background to check proximity, and is never sold or shared with third parties.

**Prayer and Visit Data**
We store the mosques you mark as visited, the date and time of each visit, and any personal "Maqam" (private places) you add. This data is private to your account and is never shown to other users.

**Mosque Data You Contribute**
If you add a new mosque or edit mosque details (name, status, accessibility information), this contribution becomes part of our shared mosque database, used to help other users find accurate information. Your name is not publicly attached to these contributions.

**Feedback and Support Requests**
If you submit a bug report, feedback, or contact us, we store your message and email address to respond to you and improve the app.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the core functionality of the app
- Show you nearby mosques and track your personal visit history
- Send prayer time notifications and adhan reminders
- Respond to support requests and feedback
- Improve and maintain the shared mosque database

## What We Do Not Do

- We do not sell your personal data to anyone
- We do not display your visit history, prayer activity, or personal information to other users
- We do not use your data for advertising
- We do not have leaderboards, public rankings, or social comparison features

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party backend infrastructure provider. Location and map data may also be processed through Mapbox to display maps and calculate routes. These providers are bound by their own privacy and security practices.

## Data Retention and Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at maqaam.app@email.com. We will delete your data within a reasonable timeframe upon request.

## Permissions We Request

- **Location** — to show nearby mosques and detect mosque visits
- **Notifications** — to send prayer time and adhan reminders
- **Background location/activity recognition** — to detect mosque visits even when the app is not actively open (used solely for this purpose)

You can disable any of these permissions at any time through your device settings, though some app features may not work as intended.

## Children's Privacy

Maqaam is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
