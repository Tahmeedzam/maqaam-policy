# Request Account Deletion

**Maqaam: Companion for Believers**

If you would like to permanently delete your Maqaam account and all associated data, you have two options:

## Option 1 — Delete from within the app

1. Open the Maqaam app
2. Go to **Profile**
3. Scroll down and tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm the deletion

Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.

## Option 2 — Request deletion via email

If you no longer have access to the app, send an email to:

**[SUPPORT EMAIL]**

with the subject line **"Account Deletion Request"**, including the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request and delete your data within 7 business days.

---

## What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

- Your account information (email, name, profile)
- Your prayer visit history
- Your personal Maqam (saved private places)
- Your earned badges
- Any feedback or reports you've submitted

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
